Philosophy

Offensive Philosophy

Power Spread with Heavy RPO Principles

I believe in an offense that is physically demanding but schematically efficient. We will establish the run, create numbers advantages through formation and motion, and put athletes in positions to make plays with the ball in their hands. RPO concepts allow us to take what the defense gives us without requiring a roster full of Division I recruits — we win with preparation, timing, and execution.

Defensive Philosophy

Multiple 4-2-5 Front | "Simple = Speed = Confidence"

My defensive system is built on a core belief: players play fastest when they are most confident, and confidence comes from simplicity and repetition. We teach Eyes-Feet-Hands at every position, install through a structured progression that builds complexity over time, and use word association to ensure every player knows exactly what to do before the ball is snapped. We confuse the opponent with presentation while our kids play fast and physical.
